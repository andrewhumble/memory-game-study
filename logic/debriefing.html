Debriefing

Dear study participant,

Thank you for your study participation. During the study, some word puzzles were solved and the level of difficulty was determined using the webcam or an adaptive algorithm. You were informed that the purpose of the study was to evaluate an already calibrated system. This statement was not true. The difficulty remained consistent. Furthermore, the video footage did not affect the difficulty of the tasks.

The purpose of the deception is to investigate the extent to which your subjective judgment differs between the different conditions. The goal is to prove a "novelty effect", which occurs when you use a novel user interface. This novelty effect often leads to a new experience when interacting with user interfaces. User interface evaluations of novel interfaces are perceived better by users although they do not differ from other user interfaces. Consequently, performance metrics and an AI-based analysis of the camera images to adjust the task difficulty were not performed. The difficulty of the tasks were ultimately not adjusted.

We apologize for incurring any inconvenience. Since this is the case that you were misinformed, you have the right to withdraw your data from the study. This means that collected questionnaires, demographic data, performance data are immediately destroyed and deleted. If you choose to do this, you will not experience any disadvantages. You will still have the opportunity to take part in the drawing of the vouchers.
Please do not discuss the study with anyone to avoid falsifying the results.
