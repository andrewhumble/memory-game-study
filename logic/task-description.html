Task Description

During the study you will be asked to solve word puzzles. You will be presented with a random sequence of letters in a blue field (see picture below). Your task is to find the correct word as fast as possible. To do this, enter the correct word by selecting the letters in the correct order. You have 45 seconds per word puzzle. After the time has elapsed, your current entry is accepted and the next word puzzle begins.

Example: The correct solution is LIFE. Select L, I, F, and E in this order.
