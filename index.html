Welcome to the Experiment!

You support our research on a system that facilitates the completion of tasks by participating in this experiment. You will either be supported by a conventional system that uses your current task performance or an artificial intelligence that analyzes biosignals from your webcam. In order to have a comparison in terms of accuracy between the two systems, a control group is included that does not use either algorithms. Which of the three groups no support through adaptation, task performance or AI-based analysis of the camera image, you will be divided into, will be told you before the experiment starts. During the experiment you will be asked to solve word puzzles.

Please read the instructions carefully. Please participate only if you have a webcam connected to your computer and when you are ready to allow the camera recording you when asked by your browser. The camera image will not be sent to us and the whole data processing is taking place on your device!
